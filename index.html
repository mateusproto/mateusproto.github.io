About

If you or your company have an app or website idea or issue, I'm here to support you through the entire process from conception until the maintenance. Exactly, it doesn't mind the size of your need, and I'm here to help you using all of my experience and knowledge in no-code platforms or full stack development with MERN.

For me, the most crucial thing will be the trust relationship we will establish during the job. You can count on a person compromised with:

  • delivery time;
  • focused on problem-solving;
  • making user-friendly interfaces (UI / UX); and
  • working well

I've been working with IT for more than 25 years and I own:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Business Management from FIAP;
  • MBA in IT Management from FIAP; and
  • Professional Certificating in Coding: Full Stack Development with MERN from MIT

I also know Agile Methods as DevOps and Scrum, and I've developed apps and websites using:

  • No-code tools for building MVPs / prototypes;
  • Express.js, MongoDB, MySQL, Node.js and PHP in the backend; and
  • CSS, HTML, JavaScript and React in the frontend
